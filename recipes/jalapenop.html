Jalapeno Poppers

Description

She spicy! For the folks who just love a kick in their cuisine, these poppers will have you wildin'!

Picante flavors are always an excellent addition to a meal. For folks looking to reserve that experience for the appetizer segment, this recipe will ensure that you hook your foodies and keep them around for the main segment!

Jalapeno poppers have always been faves and for good reason, too! The combination of flavors wins everybody over. Fresh jalapenos, some nice cream cheese, and the saltiness of bacon never fails to bring smiles to the table! Have a glass of milk handy for when these babies deliver a kick!

Ingrediences

  • 12 medium-sized jalapenos
  • .5c panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 block of cream cheese
  • 1 pack of backon (turkey bacon allowed)

Steps

  1. Preheat your air-fryer to 380F
  2. Add your breadcrumbs to a bowl.
  3. Remove the stem from the jalapenos and cut each piece in half, hot-dog way.
  4. Fill the half-pieces with cream cheese and then sprinkle bread crumbs on top
  5. Wrap a piece of bacon around the half piece. Note:You may want to cut the bacon in half if it's too much for one piece.
  6. Place in the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
