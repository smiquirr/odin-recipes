Chicken Parmesiania

Description

A burst of Italy right at home made with only the freshest ingredients... well, as fresh as they are in your pantry, I guess.

Have you ever wanted that moist, juicy chicken you can get at the Olive Garden, just without the awful lighting and interior design?

Introducing the incredible Chicken Parmesiania. Warm, tender chicken air fried to perfection topped off with freshly-made marinara sauce and shredded parmesan cheese. Spice it up a bit by making some angel hair pasta. Your taste buds will never look back!

Ingrediences

  • 2 medium-sized chicken breasts
  • 1c panko breadcrumbs
  • 1c shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1c marinara sauce
  • 2 eggs (whisked)
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasonings (to taste)

Steps

  1. Preheat your air-fryer to 380F
  2. In one bowl, add your breadcrumbs and italian seasonings while, in another, add and whisk your eggs.
  3. Lightly brush your chicken breasts with olive oil
  4. Dip your chicken breasts in the egg mixture, then the seasoning mixture, and place them into the air-fryer basket.
  5. Cook the chicken for 7 minutes at 380F
  6. Flip your chicken and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Add marinara sauce and parmesan cheese to the tops of your chicken breast.
  8. Cook for an additional 4 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
